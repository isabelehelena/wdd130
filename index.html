Pernambuco

Pernambuco is a state in northeastern Brazil, on the Atlantic Ocean. Its modern capital, Recife, includes a port, the old town and the popular beach area of ​​Boa Viagem. Known for its active and rich popular culture, Pernambuco is the birthplace of several traditional manifestations, such as capoeira, coco, frevo and maracatu, as well as holding a vast historical, artistic and architectural heritage, especially with regard to the period colonial.
